# Project Description
This Power BI project analyzes sales performance across products, categories, sales channels, and payment methods. The dashboard provides insights into revenue trends, profitability, customer purchasing behavior, and product performance, helping identify key growth drivers and opportunities to improve business performance through data-driven decision-making.
# Project Objective
The objective of this project is to analyze sales performance, product profitability, customer purchasing behavior, and revenue trends. The analysis aims to identify top-performing products and categories, evaluate sales channels and payment methods, monitor business growth over time, and uncover opportunities to improve profitability and operational performance.
# Business Questions
1. What are the overall sales, profit, and profit margin achieved by the business?
2. Which products generate the highest and lowest revenue?
3. How did product performance change between 2024 and 2025?
4. What factors contribute most to product sales performance: quantity sold or profit margin?
5. Which product categories drive the majority of business revenue?
6. How do customers prefer to pay, and how does each payment method contribute to revenue?
7. Which sales channels generate the highest revenue and growth opportunities?
8. How do monthly sales trends impact overall business performance?
9. Which products and categories offer the greatest profitability opportunities?
10. What factors are driving business growth and revenue concentration?

# Key Insights
Total Sales reached $366K, generating $34K in profit with an overall Profit Margin of 9%.
Plastic Mat was the highest-performing product, generating $21.5K in sales, while Cooking Oil recorded the lowest revenue at $12.5K.
Although Plastic Mat and Carpet Roll generated nearly identical revenue, Plastic Mat achieved higher sales volume due to stronger customer demand and greater unit sales.
Carpet Roll delivered a higher Profit Margin (19%) than Plastic Mat (15%), indicating stronger profitability despite slightly lower sales.
Product leadership shifted from Rubber Mat in 2024 to Plastic Mat in 2025, suggesting changing customer preferences and demand patterns.
Total Sales increased from $171K in 2024 to $196K in 2025, demonstrating positive year-over-year business growth.
Revenue was almost equally distributed between Online Payments ($185K) and Cash Payments ($181K), reducing dependence on a single payment channel.
Direct Sales generated the largest share of revenue, making it the primary business growth driver.
Online Sales contributed a significant portion of revenue and represent a strong opportunity for future business expansion.
Personal Care emerged as the highest-performing category, while Household and Beverages also contributed significantly to overall revenue.
Revenue was concentrated within three core categories (Personal Care, Household, and Beverages), highlighting their importance to business performance.
January recorded the highest monthly sales performance ($38K), while sales declined steadily during the following months.
Despite the decline in sales volume, profit remained relatively stable, suggesting improved operational efficiency or a greater share of high-margin products in later periods.
The combination of stable profitability, diversified payment methods, and strong category performance indicates a healthy business foundation with opportunities for further growth.
